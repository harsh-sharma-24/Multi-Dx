Pregnancies:
- Number of times the person has been pregnant
- Range: 0 – 17

Glucose:
- Plasma glucose concentration (mg/dL)
- Range: 0 – 200+

BloodPressure:
- Diastolic blood pressure (mm Hg)
- Range: 0 – 122

Insulin:
- 2-Hour serum insulin (µU/mL)
- Range: 0 – 846

BMI:
- Body Mass Index (weight/height², kg/m²)
- Range: 0.0 – 67.1

Age:
- Age in years
- Range: 21 – 81

Outcome:
- Target label (0 = Non-Diabetic, 1 = Diabetic)
